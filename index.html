Early age

Born on June 28 1971,Pretoria,South Africa. Every since the age of 10 Elon has had an interest in computers. He used this as an escape from his parents broken relationship. As his mother and father where not getting along leading to a divorce in the family just when Musk was 10-12 years old.Working with the low tech he had at age 12, he programmed his own game called Blastar. Click the button below the play! The game is very simple and shows that everyone starts out with the basics and works their way up. Once he made blastar he then sold it for 500$ using his amazing marketing skills that he learned when he was younger. You can see how he is able to use them today in many of the products that he creates. He has to sell them to others that don't understand technology. Elon's College life to First company.

At age 17 he moved to Canada to attend a University to avoid the military service in South Africa. Musk would use his Canadian citizenship he gained to easily obtain an American citizenship. Three years latter he left Canada to study physics and business at U-Penn. After graduation he attended Stanford for two days trying to pursue a PhD in energy physics. The reason he was there for two days was because of the famous dot com crash during his college days. The Dot Com crash is when the every growing tech industry hit a economic wall with the market being over saturated. Musk knew that he was not going to be able to pay for college. Balancing the odds he knew that he had to drop out. This thought comes across the minds of many college students, but be careful. Musk had already graduated and had past experience in the field. This does not jusitify the need to drop out like many other CEOs of big companies today. Stick it out and learn all that you can!

Zip2, the one to start them all.(1995-99)

After having to drop out of college and left with little money he come up with a new company.Zip2 was developed by Elon Musk and his brother Kimbal Musk, and a fellow friends Gregory Kouri. This website was described as the "yellow pages" phone numbers with locations included with each phone number. This company had a rockey start with all three of them having to live in there offices to keep costs low. They would catch a lucky break with a $3 million investment by Mhor Davidow Ventures. Using that money they would better the company by obtaining contracts with The New York Times, and the Chicago Tribune. Musk and friends continued to better the company until they where bought out for $307 million by Compaq Computer Corp in 1999.

Companies

Below are a list of companies that Musk has either help create or owns. He didn't just stop trying to make the world a better place after the first millions he made from Zip2.

PayPal

Elon and his brother Kimbal would use the money from Zip2 to create x.com or PayPal as it is refereed to today. Later to sell in October 2002 to Ebay for 1.5$ in stock.

Space X

Musk founded the space giant SpaceX, in 2002. By as early as 2008 the company was well off. With NASA awarding the company with a cargo transport contract with planned astronaut transport in the future.

Tesla Motors

Founded in 2003, with the goal to produce affordable electric cars,battery products and even solar roofs. Making its first car "the Roadster" in 2008. While today it offers the Model 3,S,X. Soon to release a new Roadster,Semi truck and even a pickup truck.

What is he up to now?

Elon Musk is still trying to better the world,with the environment in mind, with new innovations. Looking into the Solar industry, or trying to better the public transportation. Elon will never stop innovations while making our world a better space.

Solar City

Musk bought this company that his brother starter in 2006 for $2.6 billion dollars. This company works hand and hand to deliver the offer of installing solar panels into ones house. That they can use to charge a batter bank that is used to charge the owners Tesla.

Boring Company

The launch of "Boring Co." had come as a shock for the world. As a company that would reduce traffic in major cities by boring tunnels for his hyper train that would also reduce the time of travel. Even landing a deal in May 2019 for the Las Vegas convention and Visitors Authority to build the advertised loop system to move people around the convention center.
